# Spell Scroll

A spell scroll bears the words of a single spell, written in a mystical cipher. If the spell is available to your class, you can use your action to read the scroll and cast its spell without spending mana. Otherwise, the scroll is unintelligible.

If the spell is available to your class but of a higher tier than you can normally cast, you must make a Wit save (DC 10 + the spell's tier) to determine whether you cast it successfully. On a failure, the spell disappears from the scroll with no other effect.

Once the spell is cast, the words on the scroll fade, and the scroll itself crumbles to dust.
