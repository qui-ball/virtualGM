# Ghoul

*Medium undead, chaotic evil*

> Ghouls roam the night in packs, driven by an insatiable hunger for humanoid flesh. Like maggots or carrion beetles, they thrive in places rank with decay and death.

## Stat Block

| Stat | Might | Finesse | Wit | Presence |
|------|-------|---------|-----|----------|
| Modifier | +1 | +2 | +0 | -2 |

**Hit Points:** 22 (5d8)
**Evasion:** 12
**Speed:** 30 ft.
**XP:** 100

**Senses:** darkvision 60 ft.
**Languages:** Common

**Damage Immunities:** poison
**Condition Immunities:** Poisoned; immune to Presence saves against charm effects

## Actions

- **Bite.** Melee Weapon Attack: +1 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 9 (2d6 + 2) piercing damage.
- **Claws.** Melee Weapon Attack: +2 to hit (finesse), reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 7 (2d4 + 2) slashing damage. If the target is a creature other than an elf or undead, it must succeed on a DC 10 Might save or be Stunned for 1 minute. The creature can repeat the save at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success.
