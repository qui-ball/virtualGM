# Zombie

*Medium undead, neutral evil*

> Zombies are corpses imbued with a semblance of life, retaining no vestige of their former selves.

## Stat Block

| Stat | Might | Finesse | Wit | Presence |
|------|-------|---------|-----|----------|
| Modifier | +1 | -2 | -2 | -3 |

**Hit Points:** 22 (3d8 + 9)
**Evasion:** 8
**Speed:** 20 ft.
**XP:** 25

**Senses:** darkvision 60 ft.
**Languages:** understands the languages it knew in life but can't speak

**Damage Immunities:** poison
**Condition Immunities:** Poisoned

## Traits

- **Undead Fortitude.** If damage reduces the zombie to 0 hit points, it can make a Might save with a DC of 5 + the damage taken, unless the damage is radiant or from a critical hit. On a success, the zombie drops to 1 hit point instead.

## Actions

- **Slam.** Melee Weapon Attack: +1 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 4 (1d6 + 1) bludgeoning damage.
