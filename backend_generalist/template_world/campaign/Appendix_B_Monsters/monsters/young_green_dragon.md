# Young Green Dragon

*Large dragon, lawful evil*

> Thoroughly evil, green dragons delight in subverting and corrupting the good-hearted. They prefer to dwell in ancient forests.

## Stat Block

| Stat | Might | Finesse | Wit | Presence |
|------|-------|---------|-----|----------|
| Modifier | +4 | +1 | +3 | +2 |

**Hit Points:** 136 (16d10 + 48)
**Evasion:** 18 (natural armor)
**Speed:** 40 ft., fly 60 ft., swim 40 ft.
**XP:** 2000

**Senses:** blindsight 30 ft., darkvision 120 ft.
**Languages:** Common, Draconic

**Damage Immunities:** poison
**Condition Immunities:** Poisoned

## Traits

- **Amphibious.** The dragon can breathe air and water.

## Actions

- **Multiattack.** The dragon makes three attacks: one with its bite and two with its claws.
- **Bite.** Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. *Hit:* 15 (2d10 + 4) piercing damage plus 7 (2d6) poison damage.
- **Claw.** Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 11 (2d6 + 4) slashing damage.
- **Poison Breath (Recharge 5-6).** The dragon breathes poisonous gas in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in the cone must make a DC 14 Might save, taking 42 (12d6) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
