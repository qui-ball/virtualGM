# Owlbear

*Large monstrosity, unaligned*

> The owlbear's reputation for ferocity, stubbornness, and sheer ill temper makes it one of the most feared predators of the wild. There is little, if anything, that a hungry owlbear fears.

## Stat Block

| Stat | Might | Finesse | Wit | Presence |
|------|-------|---------|-----|----------|
| Modifier | +5 | +1 | +1 | -2 |

**Hit Points:** 59 (7d10 + 21)
**Evasion:** 13 (natural armor)
**Speed:** 40 ft.
**XP:** 350

**Senses:** darkvision 60 ft.
**Languages:** none

## Traits

- **Keen Sight and Smell.** The owlbear has advantage on Wit checks that rely on sight or smell.

## Actions

- **Multiattack.** The owlbear makes two attacks, one with its beak and one with its claws.
- **Beak.** Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 10 (1d10 + 5) piercing damage.
- **Claws.** Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 14 (2d8 + 5) slashing damage.
