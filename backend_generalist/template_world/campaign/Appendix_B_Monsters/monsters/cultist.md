# Cultist

*Medium humanoid (any race), any nongood alignment*

> Cultists swear allegiance to dark powers. They conceal their activities to avoid being ostracized, imprisoned, or executed for their beliefs.

## Stat Block

| Stat | Might | Finesse | Wit | Presence |
|------|-------|---------|-----|----------|
| Modifier | +0 | +1 | +0 | +0 |

**Hit Points:** 9 (2d8)
**Evasion:** 12 (leather armor)
**Speed:** 30 ft.
**XP:** 15

**Senses:** none special
**Languages:** any one (usually Common)

## Traits

- **Devotion.** The cultist has advantage on saves against being Frightened, and on Presence saves against being charmed.

## Actions

- **Scimitar.** Melee Weapon Attack: +1 to hit (finesse), reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 4 (1d6 + 1) slashing damage.
