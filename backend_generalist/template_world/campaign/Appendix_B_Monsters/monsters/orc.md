# Orc

*Medium humanoid (orc), chaotic evil*

> Orcs are renowned for their barbarism. They have stooped postures, low foreheads, and piglike faces with prominent lower canines that resemble a boar's tusks.

## Stat Block

| Stat | Might | Finesse | Wit | Presence |
|------|-------|---------|-----|----------|
| Modifier | +3 | +1 | +0 | +0 |

**Hit Points:** 15 (2d8 + 6)
**Evasion:** 13 (hide armor)
**Speed:** 30 ft.
**XP:** 50

**Senses:** darkvision 60 ft.
**Languages:** Common, Orc

## Traits

- **Aggressive.** The orc can freely move up to its speed toward a hostile creature that it can see (does not cost its action).

## Actions

- **Greataxe.** Melee Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 9 (1d12 + 3) slashing damage.
- **Javelin.** Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 30/120 ft., one target. *Hit:* 6 (1d6 + 3) piercing damage.
