# Bugbear

*Medium humanoid (goblinoid), chaotic evil*

> Bugbears are cruel and unruly humanoids that live to bully the weak and dislike being bossed around. Despite their intimidating builds, bugbears move with surprising stealth and are fond of setting ambushes.

## Stat Block

| Stat | Might | Finesse | Wit | Presence |
|------|-------|---------|-----|----------|
| Modifier | +2 | +2 | +0 | -1 |

**Hit Points:** 27 (5d8 + 5)
**Evasion:** 16 (hide armor, shield)
**Speed:** 30 ft.
**XP:** 100

**Senses:** darkvision 60 ft.
**Languages:** Common, Goblin

## Traits

- **Brute.** A melee weapon deals one extra die of its damage when the bugbear hits with it (included in the attack).
- **Surprise Attack.** If the bugbear surprises a creature and hits it with an attack during the first round of combat, the target takes an extra 7 (2d6) damage from the attack.

## Actions

- **Morningstar.** Melee Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 11 (2d8 + 2) piercing damage.
- **Javelin.** Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 30/120 ft., one target. *Hit:* 9 (2d6 + 2) piercing damage in melee or 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage at range.
