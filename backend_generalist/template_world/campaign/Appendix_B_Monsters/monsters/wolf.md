# Wolf

*Medium beast, unaligned*

> Wolves are found in subarctic and temperate regions of the world, running in packs through hills and forests.

## Stat Block

| Stat | Might | Finesse | Wit | Presence |
|------|-------|---------|-----|----------|
| Modifier | +1 | +2 | +1 | -2 |

**Hit Points:** 11 (2d8 + 2)
**Evasion:** 13 (natural armor)
**Speed:** 40 ft.
**XP:** 25

**Senses:** none special
**Languages:** none

## Traits

- **Keen Hearing and Smell.** The wolf has advantage on Wit checks that rely on hearing or smell.
- **Pack Tactics.** The wolf has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the wolf's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.

## Actions

- **Bite.** Melee Weapon Attack: +2 to hit (finesse), reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 7 (2d4 + 2) piercing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 11 Might save or be knocked Prone.
