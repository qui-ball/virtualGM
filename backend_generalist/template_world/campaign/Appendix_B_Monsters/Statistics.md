## Monster Statistics (Custom Ruleset)

This section contains stat blocks for all monsters appearing in the adventure, converted to the custom ruleset.

**Stats:** Might (STR-based), Finesse (DEX-based), Wit (higher of INT/WIS), Presence (CHA-based).

**Evasion** replaces Armor Class. **Attack rolls** use the relevant stat modifier only (no proficiency bonus). **Saves** are passive: 10 + stat modifier (not listed unless the monster has a special bonus).

**Conditions:** Poisoned, Stunned, Frightened, Restrained, Prone. Paralyzed maps to Stunned. Charmed is resisted via Presence save.
