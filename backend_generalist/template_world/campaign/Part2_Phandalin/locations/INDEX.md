# Phandalin Locations Index

Phandalin is small enough that the characters can visit multiple locations and NPCs in a single day. If the players choose to have their characters split up, they cover more ground but the GM has to take turns running each interaction. Splitting the party also makes the "Redbrand Ruffians" encounter (`../Redbrand_Ruffians.md`) potentially more dangerous.

## Faction Recruitment

Each faction has a contact in Phandalin who recruits qualifying characters:

- **Order of the Gauntlet** — Daran Edermath at Edermath Orchard (after dealing with Redbrands + Old Owl Well). Title: Chevall.
- **Zhentarim** — Halia Thornton at the Miner's Exchange (after disposing of the Redbrand leader). Title: Fang.
- **Harpers** — Sister Garaele at the Shrine of Luck (after the Banshee's Bargain quest). Title: Watcher.
- **Lords' Alliance** — Sildar Hallwinter at the Townmaster's Hall (after eliminating the goblin threat or uncovering Iarno's treachery). Title: Cloak.
