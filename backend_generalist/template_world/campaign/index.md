# The Goblin Ambush — A One-Shot Adventure

## Setup
Aldric of Corlinn Hill, a warrior, is escorting a supply wagon along the Triboar Trail toward the frontier town of Phandalin. The road is narrow, hemmed in by dense pine forest. The wagon's owner, the dwarf Gundren Rockseeker, rode ahead two days ago. Aldric travels alone with the wagon and a draft horse.

## Scene 1: The Trail
See `scene_01.md`. Begin here.

## Beats Available
- Discovery: two horses lying dead in the road, riddled with black-fletched arrows.
- Ambush: 2-3 goblins burst from cover with shortbows.
- Aftermath: tracks lead off the trail into the forest, toward a hidden cave.

## Tone
Grounded low fantasy. Goblins are dangerous, not slapstick. Combat is lethal at level 1.
