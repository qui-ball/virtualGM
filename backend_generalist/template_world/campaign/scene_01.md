# Scene 1: Dead Horses on the Trail

The trail bends around a granite outcrop. The forest is silent — no birdsong, no wind in the pines. Two horses lie sprawled across the road ahead, hooves splayed, flanks bristling with black-fletched arrows. The arrows are cheaply made, fletched in raven feathers — goblin work.

The wagon's draft horse stamps and snorts.

## What the GM must do
- Begin in-fiction.
- Describe the scene in 2-3 sentences.
- Stop and let the player decide what to do.
- Do NOT pre-empt the player's actions.
