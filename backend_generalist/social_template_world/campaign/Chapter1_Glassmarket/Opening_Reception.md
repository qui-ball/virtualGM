# Opening Reception

Glassmarket Hall is bright with brass lamps, wet coats, murmured arguments, and
the smell of wine. A records table stands near the dais with the harbor charter
laid out for inspection.

Mara arrives shortly before the vote. The hearing chair wants order, a polished
guild patron quietly gathers support, a worker delegate waits to be heard, a
junior clerk looks nervous near the records, and a temple archivist watches the
paperwork with professional interest.

Start with visible tension, not answers. Let the player choose who to approach,
what to inspect, or whether to shape the room publicly.
