# NPC Dossiers

Use these as broad vectors, not scripts.

## Hearing Chair
Council authority, public order, controlled procedure. Helpful if Mara protects
the room from chaos; resistant if she embarrasses the council without evidence.

## Guild Patron
Charm, money, confidence, careful wording. Wants the charter settled tonight and
prefers private pressure over public fights.

## Worker Delegate
Blunt worker delegate. Wants safety concerns heard before the vote. Distrusts
polite delays but responds to direct respect.

## Junior Clerk
Junior clerk caught too close to the paperwork. Knows enough to be frightened,
but needs cover before speaking plainly.

## Temple Archivist
Temple archivist. Observant and dry. Will support record integrity, but avoids
making the temple the center of a political fight.
