# Glassmarket Hall

## West Gallery
Crowded reception space. Good for public pressure, bad for secrets.

## Records Table
The charter, seals, witness slips, ink, and clerk notes are here. It is the best
place to notice whether the paperwork feels wrong.

## Harbor Map
Guild patrons and merchants gather here. Useful for learning who profits.

## Side Aisle
Workers and late arrivals linger here. Good for semi-private conversation.

## Dais
The formal center of the hearing. Speaking here changes the whole room.
