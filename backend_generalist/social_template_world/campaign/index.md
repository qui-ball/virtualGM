# Glassmarket Reception

The council is hosting a formal reception before voting on a lucrative harbor
charter. The room is full of officials, guild patrons, clerks, workers, and
onlookers trying to stay polite while everyone counts votes.

The expected charter language may have changed. Nobody wants to be the first to
turn suspicion into a public accusation.

## Use This To Test
- persuasion without automatic agreement;
- NPCs with overlapping but conflicting motives;
- secrets emerging through conversation;
- reputation, favors, rumors, and stress;
- social consequences instead of combat.
