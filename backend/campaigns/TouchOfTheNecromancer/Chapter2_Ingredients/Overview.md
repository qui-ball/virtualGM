# Chapter 2: The Three Ingredients

To break the curse, Aldric the Grey requires three ingredients:

1. Dragon scale (or impure substitute)
2. Mercury
3. Mandrake root

The player may pursue these in any order. Time pressure is central:

- Short rest: countdown -1
- Long rest: countdown -5
- Travel and delays consume additional time

Ingredient quality matters. Impure ingredients can cause ritual backfire.
