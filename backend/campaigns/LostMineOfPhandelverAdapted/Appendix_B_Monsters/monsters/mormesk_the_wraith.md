# Mormesk the Wraith

*Medium undead, neutral evil*

> A wraith is the incorporeal remnant of a particularly hateful being. Most wraiths can transform those they have slain into spectral undead servitors. Mormesk chooses not to, preferring to let the dead stay dead.

## Stat Block

| Stat | Might | Finesse | Wit | Presence |
|------|-------|---------|-----|----------|
| Modifier | -2 | +3 | +2 | +2 |

**Hit Points:** 45 (6d8 + 18)
**Evasion:** 13
**Speed:** 0 ft., fly 60 ft.
**XP:** 350

**Senses:** darkvision 60 ft.
**Languages:** Common, Infernal

**Damage Resistances:** acid, cold, fire, lightning, thunder; bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical weapons that aren't silvered
**Damage Immunities:** necrotic, poison
**Condition Immunities:** Stunned, Poisoned, Prone, Restrained; immune to Presence saves against charm effects

## Traits

- **Incorporeal Movement.** The wraith can move through an object or another creature, but can't stop there.
- **Sunlight Sensitivity.** While in sunlight, the wraith has disadvantage on attack rolls and on Wit checks that rely on sight.

## Actions

- **Life Drain.** Melee Weapon Attack: +3 to hit (finesse), reach 5 ft., one creature. *Hit:* 16 (3d8 + 3) necrotic damage, and the target must succeed on a DC 13 Might save or its hit point maximum is reduced by an amount equal to the damage taken. If this attack reduces the target's hit point maximum to 0, the target dies. This reduction lasts until the target finishes a long rest.
