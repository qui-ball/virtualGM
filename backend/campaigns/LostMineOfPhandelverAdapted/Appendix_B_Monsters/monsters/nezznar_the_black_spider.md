# Nezznar the Black Spider

*Medium humanoid (elf), neutral evil*

> Drow (dark elves) are a devious, scheming subterranean race that worships Lolth, the Demon Queen of Spiders. Drow society is strictly matriarchal. Male drow are relegated to servitor roles, and while most train as warriors, a few, such as Nezznar, become skilled wizards.

## Stat Block

| Stat | Might | Finesse | Wit | Presence |
|------|-------|---------|-----|----------|
| Modifier | -1 | +1 | +3 | +1 |

**Hit Points:** 27 (6d8)
**Evasion:** 11 (14 with Mage Armor)
**Speed:** 30 ft.
**XP:** 225

**Senses:** darkvision 120 ft.
**Languages:** Elvish, Undercommon

## Traits

- **Special Equipment.** Nezznar has a spider staff.
- **Fey Ancestry.** Nezznar has advantage on Presence saves against charm effects, and magic can't put him to sleep.
- **Sunlight Sensitivity.** Nezznar has disadvantage on attack rolls when he or his target is in sunlight.
- **Innate Spellcasting.** Nezznar can innately cast the following, requiring no material components:

  *At will:* Dancing Lights (creates floating lights within 120 ft.)

  *1/day each:* Darkness (15-ft. radius sphere of magical darkness), Faerie Fire (save DC 12; creatures in a 20-ft. cube are outlined in light, granting advantage on attacks against them)

- **Spellcasting.** Nezznar uses Wit as his spellcasting stat (spell save DC 13; +3 to hit with spell attacks). He has 8 mana and knows the following spells:

  *At will (0 mana):* Mage Hand, Ray of Frost, Shocking Grasp

  *Tier 1 (1 mana each):* Mage Armor, Magic Missile, Shield

  *Tier 2 (2 mana each):* Invisibility, Charm Person (resisted by Presence save DC 13)

## Actions

- **Spider Staff.** Melee Weapon Attack: -1 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 2 (1d6 - 1) bludgeoning damage plus 3 (1d6) poison damage.
