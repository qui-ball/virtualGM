# Grick

*Medium monstrosity, neutral*

> The wormlike grick blends in with the stonework of its lair. Only when prey comes near does it rear up, its four tentacles unfurling to reveal a hungry, snapping beak.

## Stat Block

| Stat | Might | Finesse | Wit | Presence |
|------|-------|---------|-----|----------|
| Modifier | +2 | +2 | +2 | -3 |

**Hit Points:** 27 (6d8)
**Evasion:** 14 (natural armor)
**Speed:** 30 ft., climb 30 ft.
**XP:** 225

**Senses:** darkvision 60 ft.
**Languages:** none

**Damage Resistances:** bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from nonmagical weapons

## Traits

- **Stone Camouflage.** The grick has advantage on Finesse checks to hide in rocky terrain.

## Actions

- **Multiattack.** The grick makes one attack with its tentacles. If that attack hits, the grick can make one beak attack against the same target.
- **Tentacles.** Melee Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 9 (2d6 + 2) slashing damage.
- **Beak.** Melee Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage.
