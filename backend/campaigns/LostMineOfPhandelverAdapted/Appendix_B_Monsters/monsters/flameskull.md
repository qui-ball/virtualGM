# Flameskull

*Tiny undead, neutral evil*

> Spellcasters fashion flameskulls from the remains of dead wizards. When the ritual is complete, green flames erupt from the skull to complete its ghastly transformation.

## Stat Block

| Stat | Might | Finesse | Wit | Presence |
|------|-------|---------|-----|----------|
| Modifier | -5 | +3 | +3 | +0 |

**Hit Points:** 40 (9d4 + 18)
**Evasion:** 13
**Speed:** 0 ft., fly 40 ft.
**XP:** 550

**Senses:** darkvision 60 ft.
**Languages:** Common

**Damage Resistances:** lightning, necrotic, piercing
**Damage Immunities:** cold, fire, poison
**Condition Immunities:** Frightened, Stunned, Poisoned

## Traits

- **Illumination.** The flameskull sheds either dim light in a 15-foot radius, or bright light in a 15-foot radius and dim light for an additional 15 feet. It can switch between the options as an action.
- **Magic Resistance.** The flameskull has advantage on saves against spells and other magical effects.
- **Rejuvenation.** If the flameskull is destroyed, it regains all its hit points in 1 hour unless holy water is sprinkled on its remains or a dispel magic or remove curse effect is used on them.
- **Spellcasting.** The flameskull uses Wit as its spellcasting stat (spell save DC 13, +3 to hit with spell attacks). It needs only verbal components. It has 10 mana and knows the following spells:

  *At will (0 mana):* Mage Hand

  *Tier 1 (1 mana each):* Magic Missile, Shield

  *Tier 2 (2 mana each):* Mage Armor (self, Evasion becomes 15), Burning Sphere (conjures a 5-ft. sphere of fire that deals 7 (2d6) fire damage to creatures within 5 ft.; the flameskull can move it 30 ft. as part of its action)

  *Tier 3 (3 mana):* Fireball

## Actions

- **Multiattack.** The flameskull makes two attacks with its Fire Ray.
- **Fire Ray.** Ranged Spell Attack: +3 to hit, range 30 ft., one target. *Hit:* 10 (3d6) fire damage.
