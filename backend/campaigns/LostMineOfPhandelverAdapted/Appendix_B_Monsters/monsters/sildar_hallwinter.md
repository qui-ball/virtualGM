# Sildar Hallwinter

*Medium humanoid (human), neutral good*

> Sildar Hallwinter is a retired soldier and sellsword who hails from the city of Neverwinter. He is a loyal member of the Lords' Alliance, a political organization that unites the various free cities and towns of the North.

## Stat Block

| Stat | Might | Finesse | Wit | Presence |
|------|-------|---------|-----|----------|
| Modifier | +1 | +0 | +0 | +0 |

**Hit Points:** 27 (5d8 + 5)
**Evasion:** 16 (chain mail)
**Speed:** 30 ft.
**XP:** 100

**Senses:** none special
**Languages:** Common

## Actions

- **Multiattack.** Sildar makes two melee attacks.
- **Longsword.** Melee Weapon Attack: +1 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 5 (1d8 + 1) slashing damage.
- **Heavy Crossbow.** Ranged Weapon Attack: +0 to hit, range 100/400 ft., one target. *Hit:* 5 (1d10) piercing damage.

## Reactions

- **Parry.** When an attacker hits Sildar with a melee attack and Sildar can see the attacker, he can roll 1d6 and add the number rolled to his Evasion against the triggering attack, provided that he's wielding a melee weapon.
