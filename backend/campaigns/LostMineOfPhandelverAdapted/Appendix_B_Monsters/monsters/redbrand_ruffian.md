# Redbrand Ruffian

*Medium humanoid (human), neutral evil*

> Redbrand ruffians are petty thugs and ruthless enforcers skilled at intimidation and violence. They work for money and have no scruples.

## Stat Block

| Stat | Might | Finesse | Wit | Presence |
|------|-------|---------|-----|----------|
| Modifier | +0 | +2 | -1 | +0 |

**Hit Points:** 16 (3d8 + 3)
**Evasion:** 14 (studded leather armor)
**Speed:** 30 ft.
**XP:** 50

**Senses:** none special
**Languages:** Common

## Actions

- **Multiattack.** The ruffian makes two melee attacks.
- **Shortsword.** Melee Weapon Attack: +2 to hit (finesse), reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage.
