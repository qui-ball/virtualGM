# Ogre

*Large giant, chaotic evil*

> Ogres are lazy, angry, ten-foot-tall giants that live by raiding and scavenging.

## Stat Block

| Stat | Might | Finesse | Wit | Presence |
|------|-------|---------|-----|----------|
| Modifier | +4 | -1 | -2 | -2 |

**Hit Points:** 59 (7d10 + 21)
**Evasion:** 11 (hide armor)
**Speed:** 40 ft.
**XP:** 225

**Senses:** darkvision 60 ft.
**Languages:** Common, Giant

## Actions

- **Greatclub.** Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 13 (2d8 + 4) bludgeoning damage.
- **Javelin.** Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 30/120 ft., one target. *Hit:* 11 (2d6 + 4) piercing damage.
