# Evil Mage

*Medium humanoid (human), lawful evil*

> Evil mages hunger for arcane power and dwell in isolated places, where they can perform terrible magical experiments without interference.

## Stat Block

| Stat | Might | Finesse | Wit | Presence |
|------|-------|---------|-----|----------|
| Modifier | -1 | +2 | +3 | +0 |

**Hit Points:** 22 (5d8)
**Evasion:** 12
**Speed:** 30 ft.
**XP:** 100

**Senses:** none special
**Languages:** Common, Draconic, Dwarvish, Elvish

## Traits

- **Spellcasting.** The mage uses Wit as its spellcasting stat (spell save DC 13; +3 to hit with spell attacks). The mage has 8 mana and knows the following spells:

  *At will (0 mana):* Light, Mage Hand, Shocking Grasp

  *Tier 1 (1 mana each):* Charm Person, Magic Missile

  *Tier 2 (2 mana each):* Hold Person (target must succeed on a DC 13 Wit save or be Stunned for 1 minute; repeat save each turn), Teleport (short range, 30 ft.)

## Actions

- **Quarterstaff.** Melee Weapon Attack: -1 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 3 (1d8 - 1) bludgeoning damage.
