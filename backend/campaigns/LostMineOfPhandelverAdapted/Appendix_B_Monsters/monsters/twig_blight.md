# Twig Blight

*Small plant, neutral evil*

> This skittering creature resembles a small, leafless, walking plant. Twig blights hide by rooting themselves among ordinary plants.

## Stat Block

| Stat | Might | Finesse | Wit | Presence |
|------|-------|---------|-----|----------|
| Modifier | -2 | +1 | -1 | -4 |

**Hit Points:** 4 (1d6 + 1)
**Evasion:** 13 (natural armor)
**Speed:** 20 ft.
**XP:** 15

**Senses:** blindsight 60 ft. (blind beyond this radius)
**Languages:** understands Common but doesn't speak

**Damage Vulnerabilities:** fire

## Traits

- **False Appearance.** While the blight remains motionless, it is indistinguishable from a dead shrub.

## Actions

- **Claws.** Melee Weapon Attack: +1 to hit (finesse), reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 3 (1d4 + 1) piercing damage.
