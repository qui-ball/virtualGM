# Goblin

*Small humanoid (goblinoid), neutral evil*

> Goblins are black-hearted, gather in overwhelming numbers, and crave power, which they abuse.

## Stat Block

| Stat | Might | Finesse | Wit | Presence |
|------|-------|---------|-----|----------|
| Modifier | -1 | +2 | +0 | -1 |

**Hit Points:** 7 (2d6)
**Evasion:** 15 (leather armor, shield)
**Speed:** 30 ft.
**XP:** 25

**Senses:** darkvision 60 ft.
**Languages:** Common, Goblin

## Traits

- **Nimble Escape.** The goblin can freely Disengage or Hide on each of its turns (does not cost its action).

## Actions

- **Scimitar.** Melee Weapon Attack: +2 to hit (finesse), reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 5 (1d6 + 2) slashing damage.
- **Shortbow.** Ranged Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target. *Hit:* 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage.
