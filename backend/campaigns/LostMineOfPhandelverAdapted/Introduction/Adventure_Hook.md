## Adventure Hook

### Meet Me in Phandalin (default)

The PC is in **Neverwinter** when dwarf prospector **Gundren Rockseeker** hires them to escort a wagon of mining supplies along the **Triboar Trail** to **Phandalin**.

- Gundren rides ahead with **Sildar Hallwinter** (human warrior, Lords’ Alliance ties).
- The PC follows with the wagon and oxen.
- **Payment:** 10 gp from **Elmar Barthen** at Barthen’s Provisions when the wagon arrives safely.

### Opening the session

**Default start:** Triboar Trail, a few days from Phandalin — goblin ambush (load `Part1_Goblin_Arrows/Goblin_Ambush`).

**Optional cold open:** Brief Neverwinter scene where Gundren explains the job and his excitement about “a big discovery” near Phandalin (do not reveal Wave Echo Cave by name yet).

### Character hooks

Pick one or let the player supply their own:

| Hook | Pull |
|------|------|
| Gundren’s friend | Owes him or trusts him; easy yes |
| Frontier coin | 10 gp + future work in a boom town |
| Mine legend | Rumors of lost dwarven riches |
| Faith / duty | Shrine or patron wants Phandalin protected |
| Fresh start | Leaving trouble in Neverwinter behind |

Gundren is warm but hurried — he and Sildar push on ahead to “open doors” in Phandalin.
