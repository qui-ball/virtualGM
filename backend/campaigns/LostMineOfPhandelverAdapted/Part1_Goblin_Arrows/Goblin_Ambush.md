## Part 1 — Goblin Ambush

**Level:** 1 · **Location:** Triboar Trail (`loc-triboar-trail`)

### Setup

The PC drives the supply wagon along the trail. Two horses lie dead ahead — arrows in their flanks. If the PC investigates, they recognize Gundren’s horses (and Sildar’s tack).

### Encounter: Goblin ambush

- **4 goblins** (`enemy-goblin`) hidden in brush — two melee, two with shortbows.
- They attack when the PC approaches the bodies.
- When **3 goblins fall**, the last flees toward the **goblin trail** northwest.

**GM notes:** First combat. Use `create_enemy` before attacks. Call for rolls on approach, pursuit, and captures.

### Aftermath

- **Trail:** DC 10 Wit to spot hidden path; signs of ~12 goblins and **two human-sized bodies dragged away**.
- **Captured goblin** (if knocked out): Cragmaw tribe serves **King Grol**; captives taken to **Cragmaw hideout**; “big goblin” Klarg commands the cave.
- **If PC skips trail:** Continue to Phandalin → Elmar Barthen reports Gundren missing → hook back to ambush site.

### Traps on trail (optional, one beat each)

- **Snare:** DC 12 Wit or passive 12; Finesse save DC 10 or restrained upside-down.
- **Pit:** DC 15 Wit or passive 15; Finesse save DC 10 or 1d6 fall damage.

### Story elements

- **ev1-ambush** — Goblin attack on trail. *NPCs:* — *Enemies:* goblins.
- **ev1-trail** — Trail found toward hideout. *Leads to:* Cragmaw Hideout.
- **tr1-hideout** — PC reaches cave mouth. *Load:* `Part1_Goblin_Arrows/Cragmaw_Hideout`.

### XP

**25–35 XP** for ambush and finding the trail (award outside combat).
