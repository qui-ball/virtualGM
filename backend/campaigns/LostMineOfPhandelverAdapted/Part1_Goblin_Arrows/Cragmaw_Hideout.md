## Cragmaw Hideout

**Level:** 1 · **Location:** `loc-cragmaw-hideout`

### Overview

A cave complex five miles off the Triboar Trail. Cragmaw goblins, **wolves** (`enemy-wolf`), and bugbear **Klarg** (`enemy-klarg`) hold **Sildar Hallwinter** (`npc-sildar`) prisoner. Gundren was taken to **Cragmaw Castle** for someone called **the Black Spider**.

### Key areas (abstract — not room-by-room)

| Area | Content |
|------|---------|
| Cave mouth | Goblin lookouts; stream flows out |
| Kennel | 3 wolves chained; goblins release them in alarm |
| Goblin den | 6+ goblins; crude loot |
| Klarg’s cave | Klarg + 2 goblin bodyguards; Sildar bound nearby |
| Flood passage | Optional: goblins can release water if warned |

### Klarg

- Boasts about serving King Grol and the **Black Spider**.
- If defeated, goblins scatter.
- Loot: modest coin, supplies, **Sildar’s gear** if recovered.

### Sildar

- Grateful; needs rest (short rest OK).
- Urges PC to continue to **Phandalin** and alert the town.
- **Critical reveal:** Gundren had a **map**; goblins took him east to **Cragmaw Castle** for the Black Spider.
- Promises to seek Lords’ Alliance help; may join Phandalin scenes in Part 2.

### Developments

- PC may escort wagon from ambush site first or leave wagon tied off.
- If hideout skipped entirely (unlikely), Barthen in Phandalin sends them back.

### Story elements

- **ev1-wolves** — Kennel fight (optional alarm).
- **ev1-klarg** — Klarg confrontation.
- **ev1-sildar-rescue** — Sildar freed; Gundren at castle / Black Spider named.
- **tr2-phandalin** — Travel to Phandalin. *Load:* `Part2_Phandalin/Overview`.

### XP

**50 XP** milestone for clearing hideout and rescuing Sildar.
