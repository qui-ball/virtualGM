## Part 2 — Phandalin

**Level:** 2 · **Location:** `loc-phandalin`

### Arrival

Rough frontier town rebuilding after wilderness years. The PC may deliver the wagon to **Elmar Barthen** (`npc-elmar`) at **Barthen’s Provisions** (10 gp payment) unless already handled.

If Sildar was rescued, he rests at **Stonehill Inn** (Toblen Stonehill, friendly innkeeper) and introduces key townsfolk.

### Town trouble

**Redbrand ruffians** (`enemy-redbrand`) bully locals. They wear scarlet cloaks and answer to **Glasstaff**, leader of a gang based under **Tresendar Manor**.

### Key NPCs (one scene each max)

| NPC | Role | Hook |
|-----|------|------|
| **Elmar Barthen** | Merchant | Gundren missing; pays delivery |
| **Toblen Stonehill** | Innkeeper | Rumors; room & board |
| **Linene Graywind** | Lionshield Coster | Arms; goblin raid intel |
| **Sister Garaele** | Shrine of Luck | Quest → banshee Agatha (Part 3) |
| **Daran Edermath** | Orchard / retired adventurer | Warns of Redbrands; Old Owl Well rumor |
| **Halia Thornton** | Miner’s Exchange | Offers bounty on Redbrands (optional) |
| **Qelline Alderleaf** | Halfling farmer | Son Carp knows secret tunnel to Redbrand hideout |
| **Sildar** | Ally | Pushes Redbrand cleanup; Lords’ Alliance |

Do not run every NPC in one session — pick 2–3 based on player interest.

### Redbrand street encounter

- 2–4 Redbrands harass a villager or pick a fight with the PC.
- Defeated ruffian may reveal the **hideout under the manor** or **Carp’s tunnel**.
- Leads to `Part2_Phandalin/Redbrand_Hideout`.

### Story elements

- **ev2-arrival** — Wagon / Sildar news in town.
- **ev2-redbrand-trouble** — Gang identified.
- **ev2-town-hooks** — 1–2 NPC quests planted for Part 3.
- **tr2-hideout** — Enter Redbrand hideout.

### XP

**25 XP** for settling in Phandalin and exposing Redbrand threat.
